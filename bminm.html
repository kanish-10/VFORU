Weekly Diet Plan for Males



Monday
Breakfast Aloo Paratha (2) + Raita (1 cup)
Mid-meal Fruit Salad (1 cup) + Tender Coconut Water (1 glass)
Lunch 1 cup moong dal + 1 cup bhindi + 2 chapatti + salad
Snacks Tea/ Coffee (1 cup) + Boiled Chana Chat (1 cup)
Dinner Chapati (2) + Jeera Aloo (1 cup)

Tuesday
Breakfast Chapati (2) + Daal (1 cup)
Mid-meal Fruit Salad (1 cup) + Tender Coconut Water (1 glass)
Lunch 1 cup rajma + 1 cup gobhi aloo + 1 cup cucumber raita + 1 cup rice + 1 chapatti + onion salad
Snacks Tea/ Coffee (1 cup) + Aloo Chat (1 cup)
Dinner Chapati (2) + Mix Veg.

Wednesday
Breakfast Cheela (2) + Raita (1 cup)
Mid-meal Fruit Salad (1 cup) + Tender Coconut Water (1 glass)
Lunch 1 cup chicken curry + 1 cup boiled rice + 2 chapatti + salad
Snacks Tea/ Coffee (1 cup) + Papri Chat (1 cup)
Dinner Chapati (2) + Fish Curry (1 cup)

Thursday
Breakfast Veg. Poha (1 cup) + Raita (1/2 cup)
Mid-meal Fruit Salad (1 cup) + Tender Coconut Water (1 glass)
Lunch 1 cup white chana + palak paneer + 1 cup rice + 1 chapatti + salad
Snacks Tea/ Coffee (1 cup) + Mur-mure Chat (1 cup)
Dinner Chapati (2) + Mustard Greens (1 cup)

Friday
Breakfast Aloo Paratha (2) + Raita (1 cup)
Mid-meal Fruit Salad (1 cup) + Tender Coconut Water (1 glass)
Lunch 1 cup soy bean curry + 1 cup tinda vegetable + 2 chapatti + salad
Snacks Tea/ Coffee (1 cup) + Boiled Chana Chat (1 cup)
Dinner Chapati (2) + Bottle Gourd Curry (1 cup)

Saturday
Breakfast Chapati (2) + Daal (1 cup)
Mid-meal Fruit Salad (1 cup) + Tender Coconut Water (1 glass)
Lunch 1 cup fish curry + 1 cup boiled rice + 1 chapatti + 1 cup ghia raita + salad
Snacks Tea/ Coffee (1 cup) + Aloo Chat (1 cup)
Dinner Chapati (2) + Matar n Mushroom Curry (1 cup)

Sunday
Breakfast Veg Upma (1 cup) + Raita (1/2 cup)
Mid-meal Fruit Salad (1 cup) + Tender Coconut Water (1 glass)
Lunch 1 cup chicken curry + 1 cup rice + salad
Snacks Tea/ Coffee (1 cup) + Papri Chat (1 cup)
Dinner Chapati (2) + Kofta (1 cup)
