Underweight? See how to add Pounds Healthfully

Eating nutritious foods that are high in calories is a good way to gain weight.

Being underweight can be defined in a couple of ways.

It can mean low weight for a person's height, defined as a body mass index (BMI) of less than 18.5.

It also could be weight that is 15 to 20% below the typical weight for a person's age

It is best to see your health care provider if you lost weight unexpectedly.
