Weekly Diet Plan for Females

Monday
Breakfast : Greek yogurt with fresh fruit and a handful of almonds
Midmeals : Carrot sticks with hummus
Lunch : Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, tomatoes, and avocado
Snacks : Apple slices with peanut butter
Dinner : Grilled salmon with roasted vegetables
Tuesday
Breakfast : Scrambled eggs with whole grain toast and a side of fruit
Midmeals : Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks
Lunch : Quinoa and black bean salad with diced peppers and cucumber
Snacks : Trail mix with nuts and dried fruit
Dinner : Grilled chicken breast with steamed broccoli and brown rice
Wednesday
Breakfast : Oatmeal with berries and nuts
Midmeals : Banana with almond butter
Lunch : Tuna salad with mixed greens and cherry tomatoes
Snacks : Roasted chickpeas
Dinner : Mixed Vegetable Salad (1 bowl) Stir-fry with chicken, mixed vegetables, and rice noodles
Thursday
Breakfast : Smoothie made with banana, spinach, almond milk, and protein powder
Midmeals : Hard-boiled eggs
Lunch : Grilled chicken with roasted sweet potatoes and green beans
Snacks : Fresh berries
Dinner : Baked salmon with quinoa and roasted vegetables
Friday
Breakfast : Breakfast burrito with scrambled eggs, avocado, and salsa
Midmeals : Edamame
Lunch : Chicken and cheese sandwich with carrot sticks
Snacks : Greek yogurt with honey and granola
Dinner : Grilled shrimp with mixed greens and roasted Brussels sprouts
Saturday
Breakfast : Smoothie made with blueberries, spinach, almond milk, and protein powder
Midmeals : Apple slices with cheese
Lunch : Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and cucumber
Snacks : Raw almonds
Dinner : Baked chicken with roasted asparagus and brown rice
Sunday
Breakfast : Whole grain waffles with fresh fruit and Greek yogurt
Midmeals : Raw veggies with hummus
Lunch : Grilled chicken Caesar salad
Snacks : Cottage cheese with fruit
Dinner : Grilled chicken with mixed vegetables and roasted sweet potato
