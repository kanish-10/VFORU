Weekly diet plan for high bp



Monday
Breakfast 2 paratha(aloo/mooli/onion) with 2 tsp groundnut chutney
Mid-meal 1 banana
Lunch 1 cup rice+2 chapathi+fish-tuna (100gm) curry+cabbage and green peas sabji
Snacks 1 cup boiled green gram sprouts with lemon+ 1 cup green tea
Dinner 3 chapathi+1/2 cup bitter gourd(karela) sabji+1/2 cup vegetable salad

Tuesday
Breakfast 1/2 cup Oats in 1 glass toned milk
Mid-meal 1 wedge(100gm) watermelon
Lunch 4 chapathi+1/2 cup cluster beans curry+1/2 cup capsicum paneer sabji+ 1 glass buttermilk
Snacks Avocado(50gm) whole wheat bread(3 slices) sandwich+ 1 cup green tea
Dinner 3 chapathi+1/2 cup Snake gourd sabji+1/2 cup vegetable salad

Wednesday
Breakfast 3 rice dosa+1/2 cup sambhar+1tsp pudina chutney
Mid-meal 1 pomegranate
Lunch 1 cup rice+2 chapathi+1/2 cup yam (jimikand) curry+1/2 cup ivy gourd(parmal) sabji+1 glass buttermilk
Snacks 3 Cracker biscuits+ 1 cup tea/milk(toned)
Dinner 3 chapathi(multigrain-wheat;jowar;bajra)+lauki sabji+1/2 cup vegetable salad

Thursday
Breakfast Vegetable sandwich with 4 whole wheat bread slices+cucumber,tomato, onion,lettuce
Mid-meal 1 medium size apple
Lunch 1 cup rice+2 chapathi+1 portion(100gm) grilled/stewed-tuna fish+1/2 cup rajmah curry
Snacks 1 bowl unsalted pop corn+1 cup tea/milk(toned)
Dinner 3 chapathi+1/2 cup raw banana sabji+1/2 cup vegetable salad

Friday
Breakfast 1 cup broken wheat upma with vegetables+1 glass toned milk/1 cup tea
Mid-meal 100gm musk melon
Lunch 4 chapathi+1/2 cup french beans curry+1/2 cup colocasia(arbi) sabji+ 1 glass buttermilk
Snacks 1/2 cup Sweet potato salad+ 1 cup green tea
Dinner 3 chapathi+ 1/2 cup ridge gourd(thori) sabji+1/2 cup vegetable salad

Saturday
Breakfast 4 rice Idly+ 1/2 cup sambhar+1 tsp coconut chutney+1 glass milk/ 1 cup tea
Mid-meal 1 medium size pear
Lunch 4 chapathi+1/2 cup lauki dal+1/2 cup green peas and panner sabji+1 glass butter milk
Snacks 1 small fistful (40 gm) of peanuts,almonds,walnuts+1 cup green tea
Dinner 3 chapathi+ 1/2 cup bhindi sabji+ 1 cup vegetable salad

Sunday
Breakfast 1 cup roasted oats upma with vegetables+1 glass toned milk/1 cup tea
Mid-meal 1 medium size guava
Lunch 1 cup rice+2 chapathi+aloo brinjal sabji+1/2 cup tomato dal+1 glass buttermilk
Snacks 1 cup boiled bengalgram with lemon+ 1 cup green tea
Dinner 3 chapathi(multigrain-wheat;jowar;bajra)+1/2 cup tinda sabji+ 1/2 cup vegetable salad
