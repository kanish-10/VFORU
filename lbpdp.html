Weekly diet plan for low bp



Monday
Breakfast 2 stuffed cabbage chapati + ½ cup cucumber raita + 1/3rd cup tomato chutney
Mid-meal Four raisins and four almonds and one cup of coffee
Lunch ½ cup chicken curry and two chapatis
Snacks One guava
Dinner ½ cup bottle gourd curry and 2 chapati

Tuesday
Breakfast Cornflakes and milk with strawberry (3 to 4) + 4 almonds and 3 cashew nuts
Mid-meal Two slices of toasts and one cup of coffee
Lunch ½ cup cauliflower curry and one cup rice flakes pulao and one to two roasted papad
Snacks Two chikus
Dinner ½ cup Ridge gourd curry + 2 chapatis

Wednesday
Breakfast ½ cup cucumber raita + 2 methi paratha + 1/3rd tomato chutney
Mid-meal Four raisins and four almonds and one cup of coffee
Lunch ½ cup soybean curry and 2 chapati
Snacks One cup pomegranate
Dinner ½ cup mix vegetable curry and 2 chapatis

Thursday
Breakfast 1/3rd tomato chutney + onion and cucumber raita + 2 spinach paratha
Mid-meal Four raisins and four almonds and one cup of coffee
Lunch ½ cup egg curry + 2 chapatis
Snacks One apple
Dinner ½ cup snake gourd curry + two chapatis

Friday
Breakfast 3 cashew nuts + 4 almonds + cornflakes, milk, with banana
Mid-meal Two rusks and one cup of coffee
Lunch ½ cup sambar + 2 dosas + 1/3rd cup raita
Snacks One cup of grapes
Dinner ½ cup Baked beetroot and carrot and two chapati

Saturday
Breakfast 4 raisins + 4 almonds + one cup of coffee
Mid-meal Four raisins and four almonds and one cup of coffee
Lunch ½ cup paneer curry and 2 chapati
Snacks One ripe banana
Dinner ½ cup spinach curry and 2 chapati

Sunday
Breakfast 1/3rd cup tomato chutney + ½ cup cucumber raita + 2 cup grated carrot paratha
Mid-meal Four raisins and four almonds and one cup of coffee
Lunch One to two roasted papad + ½ cup dum aloo + 1 cup vegetable pulao
Snacks One orange
Dinner ½ cup baked pumpkin and 2 chapatis
